Our Story

Building a legacy of
dignity and care.

OPL Synergy Sdn. Bhd. is a Malaysian columbarium development and investment holdings company dedicated to providing dignified, serene, and beautifully designed memorial facilities for families across the nation.

Our flagship development, the Ipoh City Columbarium, stands as a testament to our commitment — a landmark 4-storey facility set across 4 fully landscaped acres in the heart of Ipoh, Perak. We believe that every family deserves a place of lasting peace and remembrance.

Beyond memorial services, OPL Synergy offers structured investment opportunities backed by the stability of the columbarium sector — a sector marked by consistent demand and long-term value.
